# Deja

Free Personality Discovery from Your Favorite Things

Deja is a free personality discovery service that creates your personal manual from the things you love. By selecting cards across music, movies, games, books, and more, you get a quick and clear result that describes your type and suggests recommendations tailored to you. Explore Deja at [wedeja.com](https://wedeja.com/).

## Key Features

- Card-first experience: Try an interest-based personality test by simply tapping what you like.
- Personality insights: Get a one-phrase summary, core traits, and free personality diagnosis results.
- Shared experience: Compare results with friends through a built-in compatibility check.

## How It Works

1. Pick cards from 13 domains and 100+ categories based on your interests.
2. Generate your result to see your personality profile and tailored recommendations.
3. Share your result and enjoy a compatibility check with friends.

## Why Deja

Deja turns your everyday preferences into understandable personality insights without long questionnaires. It combines an interest-based personality test with practical recommendations, making personality discovery simple, social, and easy to revisit anytime.

Whether you are looking for a free personality diagnosis, new content recommendations, or a fun compatibility check, Deja helps you discover more about yourself through what you already enjoy.
